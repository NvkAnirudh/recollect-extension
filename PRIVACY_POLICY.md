# Privacy Policy for Recollect Extension

**Last Updated**: November 17, 2024

## Introduction

Recollect ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use the Recollect browser extension.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Authentication Information
- **Access Tokens**: We store authentication tokens locally in your browser to maintain your signed-in state
- **User Credentials**: We do not store passwords; authentication is handled securely through our web application

### 2. Content You Save
- **URLs**: Web addresses of content you choose to save (from LinkedIn, Instagram, YouTube, TikTok)
- **Post Content**: Text and metadata from posts you save
- **Timestamps**: When content was saved

### 3. Automatic Information
- **Platform Information**: Which social media platform you're using when you save content
- **Extension Usage**: Basic usage statistics (number of saves, errors encountered)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

1. **Provide Core Functionality**
   - Save and organize your social media content
   - Process and index your saved content for search
   - Sync your data across devices (when signed in)

2. **Improve Our Service**
   - Fix bugs and improve performance
   - Understand how users interact with the extension
   - Develop new features

3. **Communication**
   - Send important service updates
   - Respond to your support requests

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- Authentication tokens are stored securely in Chrome's local storage
- Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS
- We do not have access to data stored locally in your browser

### Server Storage
- Saved content is stored on our secure servers
- We use industry-standard encryption
- Access to your data requires authentication

### Data Retention
- Your data is retained as long as your account is active
- You can delete your data at any time
- We may retain some data for legal compliance

## Data Sharing

We **DO NOT**:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your content with other users without your permission
- Use your data for advertising purposes
- Track your browsing activity outside the extension's functionality

We **MAY** share data:
- With service providers who help us operate the extension (e.g., hosting, analytics)
- When required by law or to protect our legal rights
- With your explicit consent

## Third-Party Services

Our extension interacts with:

### Social Media Platforms
- **LinkedIn, Instagram, YouTube, TikTok**: We access public content you choose to save
- We do not access private messages or non-public content
- We comply with each platform's terms of service

### Our Web Application
- **Recollect Web App**: For authentication and content management
- **API Server**: For processing and storing saved content

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

1. **Access Your Data**: View all data we've collected about you
2. **Delete Your Data**: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
3. **Export Your Data**: Download your saved content
4. **Opt-Out**: Uninstall the extension at any time
5. **Update Preferences**: Manage what types of content are saved

## Children's Privacy

Our extension is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Posting a notice in the extension
- Sending an email notification (if you've provided one)

## Chrome Web Store Specific Disclosures

### Permissions Explanation

We request the following Chrome permissions:

- **activeTab**: To detect which social media platform you're on
- **storage**: To store your authentication token locally
- **contextMenus**: To add "Save to Recollect" to right-click menu

### Host Permissions

We access the following domains:
- **instagram.com**: To save Instagram content you select
- **youtube.com**: To save YouTube videos you select
- **tiktok.com**: To save TikTok videos you select
- **linkedin.com**: To save LinkedIn posts you select
- **localhost**: For development and testing
